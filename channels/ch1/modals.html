About Me

I believe the most powerful creations exist at the intersection of logic and emotion, strategy and artistry. My work as a financial analyst, business consultant, and creative entrepreneur is driven by the idea that numbers tell a story—and stories shape the world.

Whether administering and validating residual payment infrastructures for leading entertainment brands like HBO, Netflix, and Amazon, creating multidisciplinary projects through Coloring with Gray, or architecting holistic project frameworks for brands, my approach is always rooted in innovation, depth, and intention.

As the Founder & Creative Director of Coloring with Gray, a multidisciplinary house based on philosophy, I create experiences across film, photography, fragrance, and technology that challenge perspectives—including "Reflections of You", a multidimensional project combining fragrance, film, and product design to tell a cohesive story. My work isn't about following trends—it's about establishing new standards at the convergence of creativity, commerce, and culture.

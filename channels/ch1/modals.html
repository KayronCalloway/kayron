WHAT MAKES THIS RARE

I understand why luxury works. Not price points — the psychology: scarcity, permanence, restraint. I design price justification, not objects.

Philosophy is operational. Logic, ethics, computational thinking — actively informs how I approach meaning, legitimacy, and what responsibility looks like when intelligence is no longer human.
